How To Play Fortnite And Roblox

This works for EVERYONE who does it right.

Create an account with your school email on github.com

Click the fork button on https://github.com/3kh0/DesktopOnCodespaces

Create the fork

Go to https://github.com/features/codespaces

Click create new codespace

Select the thing you just created in the "select repository" button

Then click create codespace

Open terminal inside the codespace

Copy the code from https://github.com/Tacogamerman3/Tacogamerman3.github.io/blob/main/README.md

Paste It In The Terminal

(Location Shown Above)

Wait for it to load

When a weird menu comes up in the terminal, click INSTALL NOW.

Wait for it to load

Go to ports once it stops, even if it says some sort of error

Right click on the link and select open

Click on the orange and white lion icon (Image At Bottom)

YOU CAN ALSO CLICK THE FOX AROUND THE PRUPLE DOT ICON

Install it

Go to Geforce or Now.gg inside the lion

Open Fortnite or Roblox
